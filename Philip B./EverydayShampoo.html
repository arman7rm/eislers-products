Everyday Beautiful Shampoo

A clarifying and volumizing shampoo that helps to remove oil and product buildup, leaving hair looking glossy with a sexy, voluminous feel. A potent blend of plant-derived ingredients condition and provide antioxidant support. The result: hair that looks its lushest, bounciest best.

THE INSPIRATION:

Customers loved my Forever Shine collection so much that I decided to create a lighter version of it with an enticing daytime scent profile: fruity-floral, green and unbelievably fresh. I’d recently been on a nature walk and came across some orange flowers in bloom. I used that exquisite essence as my jumping-off point, and added a mix of other essential oils, including bergamot and hints of tangerine and vanilla. I spent months agonizing over each decision until I reached the perfect combination. The safflower oleosomes and amino acids in the conditioner make all hair types look bouncy and glossy, and the scent of the extracts is just as captivating. Even better, both the conditioning agents and fragrance are time-released to keep you feeling gorgeous.

THE FRAGRANCE:

Neroli Tangerine, Citrus Bergamot, Vanilla

HOW TO USE

Apply to wet hair; work into a lather. Rinse well. Follow with conditioner through the length and ends of hair.

  • A targeted blend of plant extracts (Rosemary, Green Tea Leaf Water, Sunflower, and Aloe) and Glycerin pamper hair without weighing it down.

  • Gently detangles.

  • Infused with the fresh, sophisticated scent of Magnolia Flower.

  • A perfect daily conditioner for the whole family.
