Peppermint Avocado Shampoo

A global bestseller, this luxuriously uncommon shampoo leaves hair looking fuller while making your scalp feel tingly-clean and refreshed. The formula is enriched with avocado extract for gentle clarifying. Crisp Peppermint Oil and 16 Plant Extracts impart a cool, invigorating sensation.

THE INSPIRATION:

My goal with these products was to create haircare that not only smells amazing, but also wakes you up, heightens your senses, and leaves your scalp tingly-clean. To make the formulas as refreshing as possible, I used pure medical-grade peppermint oil, an unusual choice for a haircare company. It took me 8 months to find the right concentration — and then, of course, the shampoo became a global bestseller. I hope you’ll give these products a few minutes to soak in, so you come away feeling energized and exhilarated, like you’ve gone from 0 to 100 with the wind blowing through your hair!

THE FRAGRANCE:

Peppermint

HOW TO USE

Apply to wet hair; work into a lather. Rinse well. Follow with conditioner through the length and ends of hair. To make hair feel even more manageable, follow with Detangling Toning Mist.

IDEAL FOR:

Oily | Flat | Fine
